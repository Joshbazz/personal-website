The Company

Bazzano Technologies Inc is a technology consulting firm helping businesses build smarter systems, make better decisions with data, and move faster with technology. We work across data analytics, algorithmic systems, custom software, and AI/ML—wherever the problem calls for it.

The name is deliberately broad. Not because we don't have a point of view, but because we've found that the most valuable consulting relationships aren't defined by a narrow service line. Clients come with real problems—messy, cross-functional, hard-to-categorize ones—and we'd rather solve those than fit them into a predefined box.

Our work is grounded in quantitative methods and built for production. We don't do demos that don't ship, or analyses that don't lead to decisions. Every engagement ends with something you can actually use.

The Founder

Josh Bazzano is a quantitative analyst and researcher with over 5 years of experience across institutional trading, portfolio management, financial engineering, and machine learning. He founded Bazzano Technologies to bring that rigor to a broader set of clients.

His background spans four companies and three degrees—from derivatives trading at Mt. Lucas Management to crypto lending at TradeStation, to quantitative ticket trading at Seatsnob, to quantitative research at Intune Analytics. Along the way, he built volatility models for 15 futures markets, managed a portfolio generating $1.1M+ in revenue, and scaled an institutional lending book by 300%.

His independent work includes a statistically validated NFL quantitative betting system (64.9% win rate, +25.6% ROI over 74 live bets), live prediction market data infrastructure, and LSTM-based price prediction models. He holds a B.S. in Finance from Penn State, completed the FinTech Boot Camp at the University of Miami, and completed Pre-MFE Seminars at Baruch College.

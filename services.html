What we offer

Services

Five core practice areas, each built around delivering measurable outcomes—not billable hours.

Website Design & Development

For businesses and professionals who need a custom web presence.

We design and build websites from scratch—no templates, no page builders, no WordPress. Every site is hand-coded, responsive, and deployed to production. You get a site that looks exactly how you want it, loads fast, and is built to last.

Custom responsive website design & development Brand-aligned visual identity & layout SEO-optimized pages & metadata Domain setup, SSL, & hosting deployment CMS integration when needed Ongoing maintenance & content updates

Best for: professionals, small businesses, and creative portfolios that need a polished, custom web presence without the overhead of a design agency.

Data & Analytics Consulting

For companies that have data but aren’t getting value from it.

Most businesses are sitting on more data than they know what to do with. We cut through the noise—cleaning, structuring, and modeling it into systems that actually drive decisions. From one-off exploratory analyses to production-grade pipelines and dashboards, we build what your team will actually use.

Exploratory data analysis & insight generation Dashboard & reporting infrastructure SQL/Python-based data pipeline design Statistical modeling & forecasting Data quality audits & remediation KPI framework design

Best for: companies drowning in data but lacking the infrastructure or expertise to use it.

Algorithmic & Quantitative Systems

For trading firms, fintech, and analytics-heavy organizations.

Quantitative systems that are built to be validated, not just built. We apply rigorous statistical methodology at every stage—from signal research through backtesting to live deployment—so you know what edge you actually have before you put capital behind it.

Quantitative strategy research & validation Backtesting frameworks & performance analysis Market microstructure & signal generation Volatility modeling (commodity, currency, index) Sports analytics & betting model development Automated data ingestion & alerting systems
Proof point: Built a live NFL betting model achieving a 64.9% win rate and +25.6% ROI over a statistically significant 74-bet live test window, then rebuilt it as fully automated infrastructure—from 4 hours/week manual work to minutes.

Best for: trading firms, hedge funds, fintech companies, or sports analytics teams needing quant infrastructure.

Custom Software & Automation

For businesses with repetitive manual processes or custom tooling needs.

Off-the-shelf software doesn't fit every workflow. We build custom tools, scripts, and systems that do exactly what you need—no more, no less. Our focus is reliability, maintainability, and handoffs that don't require us to be on-call forever.

End-to-end tool development (Python, web apps) Workflow automation & scripting Data pipeline construction & maintenance API integration & real-time data systems Internal tooling & dashboards Process documentation & handoff

Best for: businesses with repetitive manual processes, one-off tooling needs, or internal teams that need better infrastructure.

AI & Machine Learning Integration

For companies exploring AI but unsure where to start.

AI isn't magic. It's a set of tools that work well on specific problems, poorly on others, and require real engineering to deploy reliably. We cut through the hype and build AI systems that are grounded in your actual data and your actual problem—not a demo environment.

Predictive model development (LSTM, XGBoost, neural nets) LLM integration & prompt engineering Model evaluation, tuning & deployment AI-powered decision support tools Feature engineering & data preparation Model monitoring & performance tracking

Best for: companies exploring AI but unsure where to start, or teams that have experimented with AI but haven’t been able to put it into production.
